About US

Kerry TJ Logistics is Taiwan's leading provider of logistics services, and is the logistics partner of choice for many of the world's most famous brands. Kerry TJ Logistics is a member of the Kerry Group. With head offices in Hong Kong, we employ a far-reaching global network that stretches across six continents, and includes the largest distribution network and hub operations in Greater China and the ASEAN.

What We Do

Kerry TJ Logistics was formerly known as T-Join logistics Ltd., which mainly provided domestic shipping and logistics services in Taiwan. In addition to serving clients in urban and rural areas, T-Join logistics Ltd. also provided shipping support for agricultural producers in remote areas. Established in 1954, T-Join grew over six decades to establish service stations and routes covering all of Taiwan, including the offshore islands of Penghu and Kinmen. Today, Kerry TJ Logistics has over 4,000 employees and 3,000 vehicles, making us one of the most important providers of logistics services in Taiwan.

Since its joint venture with Hong Kong’s Kerry Group in 2008, the business scope of Kerry TJ Logistics has expanded to include less-than-truckload (LTL) deliveries, ambient temperature logistics services, and low temperature warehousing and distribution. In recent years, it has even expanded to include pharmaceutical logistics, building a quality brand and being recognized and valued by a broad client group. Based on a logistics network across 40 countries worldwide, Kerry TJ Logistics has constructed a diversified service platform. In addition, the company has integrated resources to provide clients in the Greater China region with one-stop services. Kerry TJ Logistics' highly reliable and flexible logistics' service is a unique advantage of the company.

Transportation Services Kerry TJ Logistics began in transportation services. Through the design of regular dispatch and precise planning of long-distance services, Kerry TJ Logistics constructed freight transportation Hub-and-Spoke Network with pickups at satellite stations to effectively and timely complete consignments and provide Business-to-Business (B2B) and Business-to-Consumer (B2C) services. So far, nearly 150 service locations have been established, including transit centers, operations offices and goods collection stations. This complete transportation network provides clients with a strong base of professional services.

Logistics Services(including ambient and low temperature storage & distribution) Logistics services are another core business of Kerry TJ Logistics. Aside from professional storage management, we strive to meet clients’ need in additional services such as devanning, warehousing, sorting, retrieving, disposal and customized value added services. Kerry TJ Logistics provides a range of logistics services based on client needs, adopting top of the line equipment such as an Automated Storage/Retrieval System (AS/RS) and has established highly efficient multi-temperature logistics centers. Cold storage and temperature controlled distribution is a key service provided by our logistics center. Unlike our competitors, Kerry TJ Logistics provides multi-temperature controlled storage: chilled (4°C), frozen (-18°C) and deep frozen (-25°C). In addition, to ensure the quality of cold storage services, Kerry TJ logistics invests in Kerry Cold Chain Equipment Company Limited that is an agent for Carrier Air Conditioning in Taiwan, providing the equipment for our refrigerated storage and distribution vehicles, making us the only logistics firm in Taiwan to offer multiple levels of temperature control and cryogenic refrigeration technology.

Sea and Air Transport, and Customs Services In order to provide one-stop services, the Kerry Group's Kerry Speedy Logistics Co. Ltd. is responsible for sea and air freight business. Through the platform of the Kerry Group, services such as international freight forwarding, sea freight, air freight, sea and air joint freight, customs clearance and courier services are provided. Container freight stations have also been established to handle the receipt, transport, assembly and disassembly of import and export goods. Kerry Freight has set up service locations in Taipei and Kaohsiung to handle customs declaration and clearance of import and export goods. Through this cooperation, Kerry TJ Logistics’ integrated logistics services in the freight forwarding and customs declaration segments have been enhanced.
